About

I am currently a 3rd-year Master student at the Institute for Network Sciences and Cyberspace, Tsinghua University. I am advised by Prof. Qi Li, Prof. Mingwei Xu and Prof. Jianping Wu. I am broadly interested in machine learning applications to security and privacy.
In September 2023, I will convert from M.S. to Ph.D., and continue my research on security and privacy under the supervision of Prof. Qi Li.
