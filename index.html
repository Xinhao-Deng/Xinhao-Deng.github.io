About

Xinhao Deng is currently a third-year Ph.D student at the Institute for Network Sciences and Cyberspace, Tsinghua University, and advised by Prof. Qi Li, Prof. Mingwei Xu and Prof. Jianping Wu.

He is broadly interested in security and privacy. His research focuses on website fingerprinting (S&P'23, SecureComm'23), malicious traffic detection (NDSS'24) and mitigation (TIFS'22).
