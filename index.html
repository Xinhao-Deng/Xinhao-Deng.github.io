Xinhao Deng is currently a Ph.D. candidate at the Institute for Network Sciences and Cyberspace, Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Qi Li. He is interested in research on the application of AI to network security and privacy. He has served as a research intern at Microsoft Research Asia, ByteDance, and WeChat.
